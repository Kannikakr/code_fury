EarthQuake
Prepare an Emergency Kit: include essentials like food, water, medications and flashlight Stay Indoors: Remain inside to avoid falling debris. If you’re indoors, stay away from windows and heavy objects. If Outside: Move to an open area away from buildings, trees, and utility wires. Provide first aid and seek medical attention for serious injuries. Listen to emergency broadcasts for updates and instructions.
Tsunami
Move to Higher Ground, Avoid the Beaches Check for Injuries: Provide first aid and seek medical help for serious injuries. Listen for Updates: Keep informed through local news and emergency broadcasts for updates on the situation and recovery instructions
Flood
Move to Higher Ground: If you’re in a flood-prone area, seek higher ground immediately to avoid rising waters. Follow Official Advice: Listen to local authorities for evacuation orders and safety information. Avoid Contact with Contaminated Water: Floodwater may be contaminated. Avoid contact and ensure that food and water supplies are safe.
Volcanic_eruption
Follow Evacuation Orders: Immediate Evacuation: If local authorities issue an evacuation order, leave the area immediately and follow the recommended evacuation routes. Wear Protective Gear: If you must go outside, wear long sleeves, long pants, a mask (N95 or similar), and goggles to protect your skin, lungs, and eyes from ash. Avoid Hazardous Areas,Avoid Ash-Covered Areas. Respiratory Issues: seek medical attention immediately.
